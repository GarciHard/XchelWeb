Our guests say:

  1. The shrimp enchiladas seemed delicious to me, the sweetness of the wine was perceived, the garlic did not exceed it; the veggies were al dente very well seasoned, the sauce tasted great without being overly choppy.

  2. Fish tacos, the best of the dish were the handmade flour tortillas, you could see the quality of the ingredients. The fish was cooked just right; the acidity of the chipotle was well balanced. Overall a well executed dish.

  3. I loved the grilled tacos since the chicken was not dry, the tortillas had a great flavor and the pico de gallo gave the dish that freshness, the cuts of the ingredients were the same size which was pleasing to the eye, the heat did not invade the different flavors of the dish.
