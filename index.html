About Us

We know the origin and quality of our products and we value the work of the people and communities that make them. We passionately promote the local, regional and national consumption of products of the best quality, as well as the knowledge and tasting of the unique flavors that are made in our country. We consider Mexican hospitality to be the other intangible heritage of our country and we work hard to represent it in our restaurants. The experience and attention of our clients extends to everything that reaches their senses, that is why we take care of every detail and try to improve through self-criticism and the opinions of our clients and friends.

All our ingredients are fresh and brought first-hand, that is why the availability varies according to theseason. In case of having that ingredient, it will be substituted.

The preparation of our food is based on the use of products of the highest quality, of natural origin, free of chemicals, hormones and pesticides, contributing to a relationship in balance with nature.

In our restaurant, we have responsible consumption, which is based on consuming less and that what we consume is as sustainable and supportive as possible.

We seek to be agents of change in the collective conscience of Mexico, we promote fair treatment and preservation and respect for the environment.
