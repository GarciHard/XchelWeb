About Us

The restaurant Xchel design was based on a global concept of contemporary Mexican cuisine.

From the beginning each stage of its creation was carefully projected, in order to build a great stage for the national gastronomy that included architecture, lighting, wine cellar, graphic design and image, setting, proposal and presentation of dishes, with the purpose of creating a total experience.

His mission was to show the greatness of Mexico through its flavors, colors and textures. The restaurant's operation was impeccable and a rigorous service ethic was observed, with the total satisfaction of its distinguished customers. A classic concept was developed with an openness to modernity. The gastronomic intelligence in the menu of dishes and special drinks included a gastronomic language and a look at aesthetics.
